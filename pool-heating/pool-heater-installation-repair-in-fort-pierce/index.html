POOL HEATING FAQ

Frequently Asked Pool Heating Questions

Get clear answers regarding electric pool heat pump efficiencies, low temperature operational limits, and local repair services.

Why is salt-air protection essential for Fort Pierce pool heaters?

Sustained port-city winds carry corrosive salt spray that degrades steel casings and copper lines; we use heavy-duty composite cabinets.

Can you repair older pool heaters in Fort Pierce?

Yes, we carry replacement parts for major brands, restoring wiring and controls to extend the life of your existing heater.

Does A/C Now LLC repair existing pool heater heat pumps?

Yes. A/C Now LLC is fully licensed to repair and service pool heat pumps across Martin and St. Lucie counties. Because pool heat pumps utilize standard refrigeration components, our HVAC technicians can troubleshoot refrigeration leaks, clean heat exchangers, and replace faulty capacitors on all major pool heater brands.
