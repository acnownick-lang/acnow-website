How do I know if my AC condenser coil needs cleaning or replacement?

In Florida's coastal environment, saltwater and humidity cause copper coils to corrode and accumulate sand. Signs of a clogged coil include high power bills, system short-cycling, or air blowing lukewarm. Regular coil cleanings remove salt crust and straighten fins, while heavily corroded coils may require a full system replacement. Use our Before/After Overhaul Slider above to see what a properly restored condenser looks like.
