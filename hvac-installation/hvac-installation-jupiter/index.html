Why Sizing and SEER2 Matter

Jupiter sits at the easternmost bend of Florida's coastline, jutting further into the Atlantic than any other point in the state — homes here take on salt air and humidity inland Palm Beach County properties don't deal with.

Jupiter's tropical rainforest climate splits into a mild, dry Nov-April stretch and a hot, wet May-Oct run where humidity pushes cooling systems hard for months. A properly installed system needs correct sizing for wet-season load and proper drainage for a low-lying city at just 10 feet elevation.
