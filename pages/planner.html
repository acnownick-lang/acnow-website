COMMON QUESTIONS

AC Lifespan & Replacement FAQ

What is the average lifespan of an AC unit in Florida?

In Florida's humid and salty air, the typical lifespan of a residential AC system is 10 to 12 years. Regular maintenance (twice-yearly tune-ups) can extend this to 14–16 years, while neglect can reduce it to 8–10 years.

How does salt spray affect coastal air conditioners?

Salt air accelerates galvanic corrosion on copper lines and aluminum condenser coils. This causes micro-leaks and structural failure. Units within 5 miles of the beach decay significantly faster. To prevent premature failure, we offer specialized coastal system options engineered to be highly robust in harsh saltwater environments.
