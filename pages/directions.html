Spot Our Billboard & Visit Our Office

Heading down I-95 or Florida's Turnpike? Look for our mascot billboard just before Exit 121 (St. Lucie West Blvd) and follow the simple route directions below!

🚗 From I-95 (Exit 121)

1

Spot the Billboard

As you approach Exit 121 on I-95, look for our giant blue-and-yellow A/C Now LLC billboard along the shoulder.

2

Take Exit 121

Take the exit ramp for St. Lucie West Blvd. Turn East (left if coming Northbound, right if Southbound) towards the stadium.

3

Enter Commerce Plaza

Drive 0.8 miles East, then turn left into the main St. Lucie West commerce park (1391 NW St. Lucie West Blvd).

🛣️ From Florida's Turnpike (Exit 142)

1

Take Exit 142

Take Exit 142 for Port St. Lucie Blvd. Head north on Bayshore Blvd to bypass traffic congestion.

2

Turn on St. Lucie West Blvd

Turn left onto St. Lucie West Blvd, head West for 1.5 miles past the stadium blocks.

3

Arrive at STE 120

Turn right into the commerce plaza. We are located in Suite 120 with dedicated client parking directly in front.
