    AC Self-Help Troubleshooting

    ❄️ AC is blowing warm air

    Check if the outdoor unit is running. Verify that your air filter is not clogged (restricting airflow). Ensure the thermostat is set to "Cool" and the fan is on "Auto" rather than "On".

    💧 Indoor unit is leaking water

    Turn the air conditioner off immediately to prevent ceiling and floor damage. A leak typically means a clogged condensate drain line. Call us to flush it safely.

    ⚡ Unit has absolutely no power

    Check your home's main electrical panel and the outdoor AC disconnect box for tripped circuit breakers. Switch them fully OFF, then back ON to reset.
