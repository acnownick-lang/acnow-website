On-Site Billing Script Helpers

Use these high-converting scripts during repair presentations to offset costs and build customer trust.

🏷️ Script 1: The Diagnostic Offset Hook When customer faces a $95 service fee + parts.

"Mr. Customer, I’ve diagnosed the problem: it’s a failed contactor. Today's standard diagnostic fee is $95. However, if we enroll you in our Comfort Club today for $19.95/month, I can waive that $95 fee entirely, plus you'll get 15% off the contactor replacement today. You actually save money on this invoice by joining the club right now."

Script 2: The Efficiency Power Waste Hook When COP Auditor shows capacity degradation.

"Mr. Customer, our performance audit shows your compressor's efficiency has decayed to a COP of 2.2. Due to the coil buildup, it's pulling extra power, adding about $220/year to your electric bill. Our Comfort Club includes two chemical flushes and tune-ups per year, which will restore up to 15% of that lost efficiency, saving you enough on power to pay for the membership."

Script 3: The Weak Capacitor Protection Hook When Capacitor Matcher shows low out-of-tolerance values.

"Mr. Customer, your run capacitor is measuring 38 mfd instead of 45 mfd. It's still running, but it's like driving your car in low gear—it forces the compressor to draw high amps, run hot, and will eventually burn out the motor. A new compressor costs over $2,500. Replacing this capacitor today under the Comfort Club plan saves you 15% on the part and protects your system from a catastrophic failure."

Script 4: The Drywall Disaster Hook For clogged primary drain lines.

"Mr. Customer, your condensate drain is backed up. If it overflows, that water goes directly onto your ceiling drywall, causing thousands in structural damage and mold. Our Comfort Club plan includes regular hot-water flushes and algaecide treatments to keep the drain clear. Plus, we'll install a safety float switch today with 15% off to auto-shutoff the system if a clog ever happens."

Script 5: The Utility Penalty Hook For systems with heavy coil buildup.

"Mr. Customer, running a dirty condenser coil in Florida is like paying a 20% surcharge on every electric bill. The layer of dirt acts as an insulator, forcing the unit to run twice as long to cool your home. The two precision coil washes included in your Comfort Club subscription will pay for the membership itself in utility savings alone."
