MAINTENANCE FAQ

Frequently Asked AC Maintenance Questions

Get reliable answers on standard HVAC maintenance schedules, active parts warranty upkeep, and our Comfort Shield Protection memberships.

How often should my air conditioning system be serviced in Florida?

Due to Florida's high operating hours, high heat, and coastal humidity, we recommend having your AC system professionally serviced **at least twice per year**—once in the spring before the summer heat hits, and once in the fall. Regular bi-annual tune-ups prevent emergency breakdowns and maintain parts warranties.

What is included in the Comfort Shield Protection Membership?

Our Comfort Shield Protection Membership starts at just **$14 per month**. It includes two 50-point precision tune-ups annually, **zero diagnostic fees** on repairs, priority emergency scheduling, and a **15% discount** on all repair parts. Members save significant money on emergency repairs.

Will regular AC maintenance help lower my monthly electric bill?

Yes. Dirty condenser coils, low refrigerant levels, and weak electrical capacitors force your compressor to work harder, using more energy. Standard maintenance keeping components clean and operating correctly can **reduce monthly cooling energy costs by 10% to 15%**.
