THE HEART OF THE TABLE

Party Ready Croquettes!

One of my absolute favorite treasures to order off the menu at my local Cuban restaurant is the lightly breaded, fried béchamel fritter platter. Generously studded with salty, deeply smoked ham, these Croquetas de Jamón completely transform a standard dinner into an unforgettable spread. Unlike their strictly Southern cousin, the hush puppy, these delicate culinary gems are fundamentally way too delicious to ever purposefully slip under the table to the dog.

A Tapas Tradition

I originally uncovered the robust magic of the croquette during an incredible, sprawling tapas crawl across Spain. There was an undeniable rhythm to ordering small, intensely flavorful plates paired perfectly alongside an ice-cold local beer or a chilled glass of dry white wine. When I returned stateside, I knew immediately that I fundamentally needed to weave that exact same relaxed, vibrant energy into my own catering menus.

"There's profound grace in preparation. In my catering days, we routinely spent entire shifts dedicating ourselves completely to building these delicate cylinders, intentionally freezing them by the dozen so we could seamlessly grab and go when the real party finally arrived."

There is a specific artistry in slowly building that rich, custardy white béchamel sauce base. You absolutely have to intimately trust the process as you generously fold in the smoked ham and patiently allow the mixture to chill. The magic reliably happens exactly in the deep fry. That brief minute submerged in hot oil perfectly binds a shatteringly crunchy golden exterior to an incredibly melty, molten core.

The Perfect Fry

When you gracefully pair these steaming golden cylinders with a remarkably sharp, heavily spiced aioli—perhaps combining rich mayonnaise with a heavy drizzle of hot adobo sauce or even substituting a brightly charged sriracha ranch—you quickly realize just how incredibly dynamic a simple appetizer platter can actually be.

Whether you're elegantly serving an expansive global tapas spread for your close friends or effortlessly passing around plates during the big Sunday game, these party-ready croquettes reliably bring a powerful touch of pure international grace straight into the heart of your home.
