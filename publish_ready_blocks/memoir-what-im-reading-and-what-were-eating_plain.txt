 In her latest thriller, Rowan Caine stumbles across a nannying post that seems too good to be true: a live-in position with a staggeringly generous salary at Heatherbrae House. Rowan is smitten by the luxurious “smart” home, but she has no idea she’s stepping into a nightmare—a haunted house sitting in a remote area of the Scottish Highlands where traditions are steeped in history.
