FROM THE ARCHIVES: CHICKEN WONTON RAVIOLI

Too much chicken? Leftovers for what feels like days? Make these on a day when your chicken overrunneth. You can store your extra ravioli in a single layer in a resealable plastic bag in the freezer, making this a recipe remix with a cheesy twist that is as efficient as it is delicious!

PARMESAN MAGIC

A cast-iron skillet. Thick slices of eggplant. A ladleful of marinara sauce and a double-topping of cheese. Take advantage of your favorite variety of eggplant using this simple frying technique to create a super Italian-inspired dish and make eggplant parm magic tonight!
