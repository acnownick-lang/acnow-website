Mornings in the Kitchen

My ghostly inspiration for this Super Supper menu is Heatherbrae House itself. This menu grabs a taste from the past and updates conventional Scottish dishes just in time for our book discussion. Choose as many or as few recipes as your group likes—but don’t forget to look over your shoulder for any shadows lurking in the corner.

Our Turn of the Key menu features Bangers ‘n Mash Hand Pies , Dill Roasted Salmon , and Fish and Chips . For the sweet grand finale, we serve a buttery Raspberry Shortbread Tart .
